Our Story

From a single camera to a full creative team

PickYourPic was founded in 2015 by a passionate photographer who believed that wedding photography should tell a real story — not a posed, stiff series of group shots, but the actual lived experience of the most important day in a couple's life.

What began as a one-person venture has grown into a close-knit creative team of photographers, videographers, and editors who share a single obsession: capturing authentic human connection.

Today, we have documented more than 500 weddings — from intimate 50-person celebrations in Mumbai apartments to five-day extravaganzas in Rajasthan palaces, from traditional Gujarati ceremonies to cosmopolitan destination weddings in Singapore and London.

We are still driven by the same belief we started with: every couple deserves photographs that make them feel the emotion of their wedding day all over again, for the rest of their lives.
